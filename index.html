Frequently Asked Questions

What is coworking space?
Coworking space is a professional client service approach in which individuals work alone or collaboratively in a shared office space.
What are the standard operational hours?
We have two shift timings. Station021 members can access the space from 9:00 am to 9:00 pm, or 9:00 pm to 9:00 am from Monday to Friday.
Can we use the Station021 as our official address?
Yes, absolutely! You can use our business address as long as you have availed of one of our membership plans.
How do we get started to become a member of Station021?
Simply check out our webpage, get in touch via a phone call or visit our premises, and someone will help you register as a member.
What are the best membership options for the small teams?
The best option for small teams is to take Dedicated Desks in our Coworking area or a small Private Office that is good for 2 – 3 team members.
Can I stop to check out the space? Or do I need book a tour?
It would be best to schedule tours in advance through the following link; https://station021.com/book-a-tour/ or contact us directly on our
WhatsApp Number.
Can we have specific dedicated desks?
Yes absolutely! We will try and accommodate any requests made as long as unassigned desks are available at the requested locations.
How can we update the information in your records?
Please get in touch with our Operational Manager or email us with your desired changes at info@station021.com
What are the different types of membership plans at Station021?
Station021 offers three memberships – Dedicated Desk, Private Offices, and Virtual Office.
What type of people work at Station021?
We have a variety of professionals, creatives, entrepreneurs, and freelancers that work out of the coworking space.
How do conference room credits work? How can I book a time for using a conference room?
Depending on their membership subscription, all members get a certain amount of complimentary access hours for bookable rooms. Bookings
can be made through our online portal.
Do you encourage and provide networking opportunities?
Yes, as a coworking space, encourage and hope that anyone who works out of here gets to make the best connections in their field.
I'd like to host an event at Station021. What are the next steps?
Please get in touch with our Operational Team at info@station021.com
